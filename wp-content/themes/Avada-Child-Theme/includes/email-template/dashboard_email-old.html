Gateway has always been good looking, but this fresh look paired with new tools has Gateway gleaming. The features you rely on are still there, we’ve just added a little more to love.

Custom Checkout Button in Seconds

This release offers a checkout button widget that lets you create a custom checkout button or form for your website in seconds.
  1. Log in to Dashboard.
  2. Answer a few easy questions and watch the form build itself.
  3. Test it as you go to see how you like it.
  4. Copy/paste the form code that’s automatically created once you’ve picked the form you like best.
  5. Hit refresh and boom—it appears.
That's it. Nothin' to it!

All the Integrated Payments, None of the Extra Costs

Add as many custom checkout buttons or forms to your websites as you like at no additional cost! Once it’s live, payments processed via the checkout button or form will show up in your existing Dashboard. This keeps your payments information in one spot and saves you from those painful reconciliation efforts.
